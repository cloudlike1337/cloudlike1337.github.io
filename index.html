About

I'm a student at KMH (Royal College of Music) in Stockholm, where my passion for music production and sound design takes center stage.

My specialization lies in crafting immersive audio experiences for video games. I thrive on the challenge of creating soundscapes that bring virtual worlds to life, enhancing gameplay and storytelling.

However, my musical journey doesn't stop there. I have a deep appreciation for all forms of music and enjoy experimenting with various genres and styles. Collaboration is another facet of my artistic journey, and I relish the opportunity to work with fellow artists, merging our unique talents to create something extraordinary.

Whether it's crafting sound effects and foley or composing an intricate soundtrack, I'm driven by the desire to push boundaries and be a part of discovering what sound has the power to achieve.
