About

My name is Nils Jobs, and I'm currently studying music production at KMH (Royal College of Music) in Stockholm, where my passion for music production and sound design takes center stage.

All of my life I've loved video games, and its sounds. I thrive on the challenge of creating soundscapes that bring virtual worlds to life, enhancing gameplay and storytelling. I also write and produce music in collaboration with song writers and artists.

I have a deep appreciation for all forms of music and enjoy experimenting with various genres and styles. I always relish the opportunity to work with other creative minds, merging our skills to try and go above and beyond.

Whatever the creative task at hand may be, I'm always driven by the desire to push boundaries, explore and discover what sound has the power to achieve.
