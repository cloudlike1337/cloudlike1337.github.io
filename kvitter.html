Kvitter - Arcade Game

‎

‎

Sign up for the unique dating app "Kvitter," doubling as the name of this rhythm/dancing game, and a dating app where you embark to look for the bird of your dreams.

In this project, I had the opportunity to provide sound effects and music to create a playful audio environment as the player gets to become a bird, and experience what it would be like if humans chose their partners like birds do.

"Kvitter" was a student project I was invited on to, that allowed me to explore the intersection of sound, gameplay, and storytelling. It was incredible to see the effort of the team recognized with awards for "Best Presentation" and "Best in Show" at GGC 2023.
