Kvitter - Arcade Game

Sign up for the unique world of "Kvitter," a bird dating and dancing simulator. In this project, I had the opportunity to create an immersive and playful audio environment that enhances the player's experience as they become the bird and groove to the beat.

"Kvitter" was a student project I was invited on to, that allowed me to explore the intersection of sound, gameplay, and storytelling. It was incredible to see the effort of the team recognized with awards for "Best Presentation" and "Best in Show" at the GGC Show Awards. Click the image to listen.

(Due to the nature of arcade games, there is currently no playable version of the game.)
